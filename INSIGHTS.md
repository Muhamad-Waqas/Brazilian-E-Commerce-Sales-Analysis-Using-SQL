# Business Insights

This file contains the key insights discovered from the Brazilian E-Commerce
Sales Analysis project using SQL.

The analysis focuses on sales performance, customer behavior, product
performance, and geographic trends.

---

# 1. Sales Analysis Insights

## Highest Revenue Year

### Finding:
2018 generated the highest revenue compared to previous years.

### Insight:
The business showed growth over time, with increasing sales activity after
the initial years. This indicates that the platform gained more customers and
order volume as it became more established.

---

## Lowest Sales Month

### Finding:
The lowest sales occurred during the early period of the dataset.

### Insight:
Lower sales in the initial period may indicate that the business was still
growing and had fewer customers compared to later years.

---

## Monthly Sales Trend

### Finding:
Monthly sales show an increasing trend with some fluctuations.

### Insight:
The overall growth indicates improving business performance, while monthly
changes show that customer demand can vary over time.

---

# 2. Customer Analysis Insights

## Repeat Customers

### Finding:
The number of repeat customers is lower than customers who purchased only once.

### Insight:
Most customers made only a single purchase, which indicates that customer
retention is an opportunity for improvement.

The business can focus on strategies to encourage customers to make repeat
purchases.

---

## Average Customer Spending

### Finding:
Customers have different spending levels, with some customers contributing
more revenue than others.

### Insight:
Understanding customer spending behavior helps identify valuable customers
and customer segments.

---

## High Revenue Customers

### Finding:
A smaller group of customers contributes a larger portion of revenue.

### Insight:
High-value customers are important for business growth and can be targeted
with personalized offers and loyalty programs.

---

# 3. Product Analysis Insights

## Top Revenue Product Categories

### Finding:
The Beleza_Saude category generated the highest revenue among all product
categories.

### Insight:
This category has strong customer demand and is an important contributor to
overall sales.

---

## Lowest Revenue Product Categories

### Finding:
Some product categories generated significantly lower revenue compared to
top-performing categories.

### Insight:
Low-performing categories may need further analysis to understand whether the
reason is low demand, limited products, or customer preference.

---

## Average Price by Category

### Finding:
Product categories have different average price levels.

### Insight:
Different categories have different pricing patterns, which can help the
business understand customer purchasing behavior.

---

## Popular Categories by Region

### Finding:
Different regions show different preferences for product categories.

### Insight:
Regional product preferences can help businesses understand customer demand
and improve product planning.

---

# 4. Geographic Analysis Insights

## Highest Revenue State

### Finding:
São Paulo (SP) generated the highest revenue with approximately 5.2 million
in sales.

### Insight:
São Paulo is the strongest market in this dataset and contributes significantly
to overall business revenue.

---

## Lowest Revenue State

### Finding:
RR generated the lowest revenue compared to other states.

### Insight:
Lower-performing states may have opportunities for growth by improving customer
reach and market presence.

---

## Highest Customer State

### Finding:
São Paulo (SP) has the highest customer base with approximately 41,375
customers.

### Insight:
Customer activity is highly concentrated in São Paulo, making it the most
important region for the business.

---

## Top Cities by Revenue

### Finding:
São Paulo city generates significantly higher revenue compared to other cities.

### Insight:
Major cities are the main contributors to e-commerce revenue in this dataset.

---

## Top Cities by Customer Count

### Finding:
São Paulo city has the highest customer count with approximately 15,402
customers.

### Insight:
Large cities have higher customer activity, showing that urban areas are
important markets for e-commerce growth.

---

# Overall Project Insights

- Revenue increased over time, showing business growth.
- Customer retention is an area where the business can improve because most
  customers purchased only once.
- Some product categories contribute significantly more revenue than others.
- Sales and customers are highly concentrated in major regions, especially
  São Paulo.
- SQL analysis helps identify important patterns in customer behavior,
  products, and regional performance.
